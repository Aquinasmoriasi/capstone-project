CLIMATE CHANGE
GLOBAL SUMMIT
2022

This is a 3-day summit about the climate change crisis effects and why they matter. It will take place in Mombasa, Kenya in November.

Mon, 21st November 2022

@The Koblenz Hall Mombasa

Main Program

  • Lecture Listen to the speakers from various countries about their take on climate change.
  • Exhibition Check out a gallery about the devastating effectsof climate change around the world.
  • Forum Share your thoughts about the presentation and your views on what can be done to avert climate change.
  • Connect Get the chance to speak to various people from all kinds of backgrounds.
